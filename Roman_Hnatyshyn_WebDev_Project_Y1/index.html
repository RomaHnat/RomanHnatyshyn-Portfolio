Explore our Leisurements!

The rooftop swimming pool at Hnat B&B is a true gem. The pool area is designed with comfortable lounge chairs and umbrellas, providing the perfect spot to enjoy a good book or catch up on some work while taking in the stunning scenery. The pool itself is well-maintained and cleaned regularly, ensuring a safe and enjoyable swimming experience for all. In the evenings, the rooftop pool area transforms into a magical space with beautiful lighting and a cozy atmosphere. It's the perfect place to wind down after a busy day of exploring the city, and to enjoy a glass of wine or a cocktail while taking in the stunning views of the city skyline.
